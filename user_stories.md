# Smart Clinic Management System - User Stories

### 1. Admin Portal
* **As an Admin**, I want to register a new doctor with their specialty and availability details so they can start accepting patient visits.

### 2. Patient Portal
* **As a Patient**, I want to register/login securely and search for available doctors by their name or medical specialty.
* **As a Patient**, I want to select an available time slot and book an appointment with a chosen doctor.

### 3. Doctor Portal
* **As a Doctor**, I want to log in and view a comprehensive list of all my patient appointments for the current day.
* **As a Doctor**, I want to write and submit a prescription for a patient during or after an appointment slot.
